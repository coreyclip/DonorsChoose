About Your Results

Overall about 84% of all applications on DonorsChoose.org eventually get funded before they expire in 4 weeks. Most projects on DonorsChoose.org are between <$1K and $4K. Requesting lower amounts of money increases the likelyhood of a project eventually getting funding. Interestingly our research does show that projects with high standard deviations in the prices of items requested is associated with a lower chance of getting funded.
